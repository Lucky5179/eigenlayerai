‘Infinite Layers’ Podcast

Watch ‘Infinite Layers’ Podcast
Infinite Layers is a developer-focused video educational series where we invite ecosystem projects to showcase their projects. Format will be an introduction, a 15-30 minute presentation, and a Q & A with questions from the host and the audience.
Videos will be live streamed to Twitter and YouTube and then available on the Infinite Layers playlist on the EigenLayer YouTube account.
