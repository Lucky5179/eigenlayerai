Understanding the EdgeLayerAI Ecosystem
Restakers form the backbone of EdgeLayerAI by providing shared security through Ethereum and other ERC-20 tokens.

Actively Validated Services (AVSs) harness the power of restaked ETH and other ERC-20 tokens to enhance their capabilities.

Rollups benefit from innovative modular services offered by AVSs, such as the hyperscale data availability provided by EigenDA.

Operators are responsible for validation tasks that AVSs depend on for their operation.
